## Content

**Better Place** raised $850M committed to battery swapping:
- Specific commitment *attracted* believers
- Believers *filtered out* skeptics
- When market feedback indicated alternative approaches, no skeptics remained to advocate pivoting
- **Result**: Liquidation (2013)

**Tesla** committed to a *mission* ("accelerating sustainable transport"), not a product:
- Mission-level commitment *preserved* flexibility
- Flexibility *enabled* pivots across vehicle segments, charging, and energy storage
- **Result**: Market leadership

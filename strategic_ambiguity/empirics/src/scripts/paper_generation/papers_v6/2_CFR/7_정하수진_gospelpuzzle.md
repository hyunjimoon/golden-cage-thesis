## Â¶7 Gospel: The Capital-Enables-Learning View

Capital *enables* experimentation (Kerr, Nanda, & Rhodes-Kropf, 2014; Camuffo et al., 2020). Experimentation *enables* learning. Learning *enables* repositioning. This logic predicts dR/dF > 0.

## Â¶8 Puzzle: The Empirical Contradiction

Yet the data show dR/dF < 0. A 1SD increase in funding *correlates with* 0.4SD less repositioning. Well-funded ventures adapt *less*, not more. Why?

Cash is a double-edged sword:
- **Helps** sampling (more experiments)
- **Harms** recalibrating and reframing (locked into commitments)

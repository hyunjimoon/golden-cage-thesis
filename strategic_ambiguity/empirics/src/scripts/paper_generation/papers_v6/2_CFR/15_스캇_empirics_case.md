## Case 1: Segway

Segway raised $100M+ committed to a **specific production architecture**: gyroscopic two-wheel platform as THE solution for personal transportation.

**The cage formed because**:
- **Operational lock-in**: $100M invested in gyroscopic manufacturing, proprietary battery systems
- **Governance homogeneity**: Celebrity investors (Jeff Bezos, John Doerr) all believed in the specific two-wheel form factor
- **Signal blindness**: When market feedback indicated warehouse logistics as viable application, no governance voice *advocated* pivoting

The pivot to warehouse logistics (Segway Robotics) occurred only after near-failure.

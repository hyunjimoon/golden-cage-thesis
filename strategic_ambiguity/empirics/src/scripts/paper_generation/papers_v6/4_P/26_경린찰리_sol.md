## Phase 1: Segment and Collaborate (NAIL IT)

**Marketing (Segment)**: Maintain optionality across segments until market signals clarify:
- Robotaxi (high uncertainty, consumer-facing)
- Trucking (medium uncertainty, B2B)
- Last-mile delivery (lower uncertainty, controlled environments)

**Operations (Collaborate)**: Develop through partnerships that preserve flexibility:
- Delivery capability: Partner with Uber, Lyft
- Production (Atoms): Collaborate with OEMs (Hyundai, Toyota)
- Production (Bits): Partner with AI startups for perception/planning

---

## Phase 2: Replicate, Processify, Capitalize (SCALE IT)

Following segment validation:
- **Replicate**: Extend proven approaches to new geographies (CA ‚Üí TX ‚Üí international)
- **Processify**: Convert ad-hoc practices into repeatable processes
- **Evaluate**: Establish metrics; pursue aggressive capitalization only after operational validation

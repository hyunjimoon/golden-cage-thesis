## Capitalize: Strategic Ambiguity

Intentional vagueness enables:
- Collaborators project their own optimism
- Investors with different beliefs can participate
- Avoids premature operational lock-in

**NOT**: Vague vision
**BUT**: Vague operational specifics with clear direction

---

## Segment: Market Clarity

| Segment | Uncertainty | Signal Quality |
|:--------|:------------|:---------------|
| Robotaxi | High | Low |
| Trucking | Medium | Medium |
| Last-mile delivery | Lower | Higher |

Track signal quality across segments. Move resources toward clearer signals.

# Figure Color Palette - Science Journal Standard

## Design Philosophy
> "단순함을 미적가치로, 우아하고 직관적이며 100년 지나도 잊히지 않는 디자인"

Inspired by: Edward Tufte, Nature, Science, Cell

## Primary Palette (3 Colors)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Forest Green** | `#1E5631` | Positive effects, growth, success, Movers |
| **Dark Red** | `#8B2332` | Negative effects, constraints, warnings |
| **Medium Gray** | `#6B6B6B` | Neutral/baseline data, Stayers |

## Secondary Palette (Grayscale)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Black** | `#1A1A1A` | Text, primary lines |
| **Dark Gray** | `#4A4A4A` | Secondary elements |
| **Light Gray** | `#D0D0D0` | Gridlines, backgrounds |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Background |

## Special: Bluebird Blue (파랑새)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Bluebird Blue** | `#6BA3D6` | B variable, strategic breadth |

Chosen for its calming, optimistic quality - represents the "freedom" of strategic flexibility.

## Semantic Mapping

| Variable | Color | Hex | Rationale |
|----------|-------|-----|-----------|
| E (Early funding) | Dark Red | `#8B2332` | Creates "cage" = constraint |
| R (Repositioning) | Forest Green | `#1E5631` | Enables growth = positive |
| G (Growth) | Forest Green | `#1E5631` | Success outcome |
| B (Breadth) | Bluebird Blue | `#6BA3D6` | Strategic flexibility |
| Stayer | Medium Gray | `#6B6B6B` | Neutral/static |
| Mover | Forest Green | `#1E5631` | Dynamic/positive |

## Figure-Specific Guidelines

### Fig 1: Capital Paradox
- Scatter points: Medium Gray (`#6B6B6B`)
- Regression line: Dark Red (`#8B2332`)

### Fig 2: B Trajectories by Archetype
- Background paths: Light Gray (10% opacity)
- Zoom-in trajectory: Bluebird Blue (`#6BA3D6`)
- Stayer trajectory: Medium Gray (`#6B6B6B`)
- Zoom-out trajectory: Forest Green (`#1E5631`)

### Fig 3: Golden Cage Mechanism
- E → R arrow: Dark Red (`#8B2332`) - suppresses
- R → G arrow: Forest Green (`#1E5631`) - enables

### Fig 4: Growth by R (Mover Advantage)
- Mover bars: Forest Green (`#1E5631`)
- Stayer bars: Dark Red (`#8B2332`)

### Fig 5: Industry ρ(E,G)
- All bars: Grayscale only (no color accents)
- This is NOT a Mover vs Stayer comparison

### Fig 6-8: Data Figures
- Mover/Positive: Forest Green (`#1E5631`)
- Stayer/Negative: Dark Red (`#8B2332`)
- Neutral/baseline: Medium Gray (`#6B6B6B`)

## Typography
- Font: Helvetica Neue or Arial
- Title: 14pt Bold
- Labels: 11pt Regular
- Annotations: 10pt

## Grid
- Major grid: Light Gray (#D0D0D0), 0.5pt
- Minor grid: None (remove for cleanliness)

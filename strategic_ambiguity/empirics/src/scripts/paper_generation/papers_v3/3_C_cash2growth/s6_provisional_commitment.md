### The Pattern

Movers (Tesla, Aurora, Waymo) used provisional commitments to navigate uncertainty. They started with broad visions, made revisable bets, and adapted based on evidence. Stayers (Cruise, TuSimple, Motional) made rigid commitments earlyâ€”locking themselves into specific technologies, markets, or timelines before the industry had stabilized.

### Key Insight

> **Movement requires Provisional Commitment.**
> Rigid commitment enables funding but blocks adaptation.
> Stayers are trapped not by lack of vision, but by rigid commitment to it.

The difference between success and failure is not the initial visionâ€”it is the commitment type. Rigid commitments create the Fund2Cage mechanism (Module M2). Provisional commitments create the escape route.
